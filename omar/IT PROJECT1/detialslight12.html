Case GIGABYTE C200 GLASS

description:

The Gigabyte C200 Glass is a sophisticated mid-tower PC case designed for gamers and creators who appreciate a clean, modern aesthetic. Featuring a stunning black tempered glass front and side panel, it allows a clear view of your internal components while maintaining a sleek profile. The front panel is accented with a stylish RGB LED strip that adds a touch of light without being overwhelming. Built with high-quality materials and an optimized airflow design, this case provides the perfect balance between professional looks and high-performance cooling.

Specifications:

  • Case Form Factor: Mid Tower.
  • Materials: Steel, Plastic, and Tempered Glass (Front & Side).
  • Motherboard Support: Mini-ITX, Micro-ATX, and ATX.
  • I/O Panel: 2x USB 3.0, RGB LED Switch, Audio In/Out.
  • Lighting: Integrated RGB LED strip on the front panel.
  • Drive Bays: 2x 3.5" Internal, 2x 2.5" Internal.
  • Cooling System Support:
    • Front: 3x 120mm / 2x 140mm fans.
    • Top: 2x 120mm fans.
    • Rear: 1x 120mm fan (Pre-installed).
  • Radiator Support: Up to 280mm (Front) / 120mm (Rear).
  • Dust Filters: Removable magnetic filters on top and bottom for easy cleaning.
  • Max GPU Length: 330 mm.
