Airline W6 184QAX Access point Wifi

description:

The Airline W6 184QAX is a high-performance Wi-Fi 6 Access Point
designed to deliver lightning-fast internet speeds and exceptional
coverage. Leveraging the latest 802.11ax technology, it provides a stable
and efficient connection for multiple devices simultaneously, making it
ideal for smart homes and high-traffic environments. With its four high-gain
external antennas, it minimizes dead zones and ensures a strong signal across
every corner of your space. Its sleek, matte black design fits seamlessly into
any modern setup, offering both reliability and style.

product price

Specifications:

  • Wireless Standard: Wi-Fi 6 (IEEE 802.11ax/ac/n/a 5 GHz, IEEE 802.11ax/n/b/g 2.4 GHz).
  • Data Rate: Dual-band speeds up to 1800 Mbps (1200 Mbps on 5 GHz + 600 Mbps on 2.4 GHz).
  • Antennas: 4x High-Gain external omni-directional antennas.
  • Core Technology: Supports MU-MIMO and OFDMA for efficient data transmission to multiple devices.
  • Security: Advanced WPA3 encryption for enhanced network protection.
  • Interface: 1x Gigabit WAN port, 3x Gigabit LAN ports (for high-speed wired connections).
  • Processor: High-performance Quad-core CPU for seamless multitasking.
  • Coverage: Intelligent Beamforming technology to focus signal strength toward connected devices.
  • Compatibility: Fully backward compatible with Wi-Fi 5 (802.11ac) and older standards.
